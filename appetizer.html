Bruschetta

Ingredients:

  • 1 baguette, sliced
  • 2 large tomatoes, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400째F (200째C).
  2. Arrange the baguette slices on a baking sheet and toast for 5-7 minutes until golden.
  3. In a bowl, mix diced tomatoes, minced garlic, olive oil, basil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Top each toasted baguette slice with the tomato mixture.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • 12 large button mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375째F (190째C).
  2. Remove stems from mushrooms and chop them finely.
  3. In a bowl, mix chopped stems, breadcrumbs, garlic, Parmesan, parsley, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spoon the mixture into mushroom caps.
  5. Place stuffed mushrooms on a baking sheet and bake for 20 minutes until golden.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy!
